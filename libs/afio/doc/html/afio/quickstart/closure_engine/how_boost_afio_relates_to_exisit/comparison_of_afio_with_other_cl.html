Comparison of AFIO with other Closure Execution Engines

The following tables illustrate how Boost.AFIO relates to some proposed closure execution engines. These proposals are related to the internal implementations of such systems at Google and Microsoft. N3378 was Google's initial proposal for such an engine, and its successor, N3562 was a joint effort between Microsoft and Google. Anyone wishing to compare Boost.AFIO to an engine related to one of these proposals should find the following tables useful.
